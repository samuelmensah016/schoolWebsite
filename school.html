Boarding

Our boarding houses provide a supportive environment that nurtures student's academic, emotional, and

spiritual growth. With caring houseparents overseeing their welfare, students enjoy a related and homely

atmosphere while collaborating with other scholars. They gain a strong sense of identity and develop

lifelong relationships that enrich their educational experienc.
