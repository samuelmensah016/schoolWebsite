KIDDIES

Welcome to Everest Crest School's Kiddies Program! Our program is designed to provide a safe and nurturing environment where young children can learn and grow. Our experienced teachers and caregivers are dedicated to creating a fun and engaging atmosphere that will encourage your child's natural curiosity and love of learning.

At Everest Crest School's Kiddies Program, we believe that every child is unique and learns differently. That's why we offer a variety of learning opportunities that we tailored to your Child's individual needs and interests. Our program includes hands-on activities, imaginative play, music and movement, language and literacy, and much more.

We understand that choosing the right school for your child is an important decision, and we want you to feel confident and reassured that your child is in good hands. That's why we prioritize communication and collaboration with parents to ensure that we are meeting the needs of your child and family.

Here are some benefits your child will gain from our Kiddies Program:
  • Development of social skills through play and interaction with peers
  • Enhanced cognitive abilities through various stimulating activities
  • Improved communication skills through language and literacy activities
  • Development of self-confidence and self-esteem through positive reinforcement and praise
  • A safe and nurturing environment where your child will thrive and grow

Enroll your child in Everest Crest School's Kiddies Program today and give them the gift of a strong foundation for

their future academic success
